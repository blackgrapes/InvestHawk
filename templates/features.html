InvestHawk Trading Strategy

Smart trading strategies for NSE F&O markets

Universe

We focus only on NSE F&O (Futures and Options) stocks, dynamically updating the list of eligible stocks daily.

Entry Criteria

  • Enter a trade when a stock opens for trading after hitting a circuit limit.
  • Track stocks that resume trading after the circuit limit is hit.
  • If a stock breaks an upper circuit, enter a long position; if it breaks a lower circuit, enter a short position.

Exit Criteria

  • Exit when the stock reaches the next circuit limit (upper or lower).
  • Example: If entered at an upper circuit, exit at the next upper or lower circuit.

Risk Management

  • Limit exposure with adjustable client preferences.
  • Trail stop-losses at 0.30% when the stock jumps 1%.
  • Initial stop-loss set at 0.50%.

Filters & Constraints

  • Strategy operates only during market hours (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM).
  • Only intraday trading is allowed (MIS, not CNC).

Execution Logic

  • Monitor circuit limits in real-time.
  • Fetch circuit limit data and execute trades using Angel One API.
